Note that to join the training you must have a sports card. There is an opportunity for students without sports cards to buy a limited amount of daily cards. Please contact the SSC for more information.

Members who have a yellow belt or higher are expected to wear a dobok (traditional taekwondo clothing). Members with a white belt may wear long trousers such as a pair of sweatpants and a t-shirt. Please wear flipflops or some other kind of footwear when walking from the changing room to the training hall, so we can train in a clean hall.

New member?

If you are interested in taekwondo you are welcome to join a training. For the first few trainings you attend you don't have to be registered as a member. Once you have decided that you want to continue training with us you have to register and pay the membership fee of €12 for a year, which can be done with any board member. You can fill out the registration form.

Training program

All sorts of exercises are embedded in the trainings, taking place two times a week led by our first dan black belt trainer. A training is more or less constructed as follows:

  • Warming up: Running, jumping, power or balance-exercises combined with practising reflexes.
  • Flexibility; you will be amazed by your own flexibility as a result of the extensive stretching routines!
  • Kicking techniques: different kicking techniques are trained regularly, rotated with the training of several muscle groups such as legs, arms/chest and stomach/back
  • The remaining content of every training differs: sparring, self-defence, combination kicks, techniques and endurance
  • Cooling down; often sparring, kicking or punching circuits are concluded with a cooling down. When finished we often hang around in the training hall for some time before meeting up again in sports canteen!
