Trainers

Our trainers are Merijn Donders and Melanie Ahn.

Merijn Donders

I have been practising martial arts since 1988 when my father placed me at judo (1988-1995). After a lot of training and competitions I decided to continue this passion in taekwondo (1995-present). Since 1998 I am the proud owner of my 1st degree black belt in taekwondo. Through the years I grew into the role of assistant trainer and had been assisting the previous Ilyeo trainer (Redouane) during busy trainings. I regularly taught a class and since January 2011 I am the head trainer at taekwondo Ilyeo. I teach on Thursdays and (often) the advanced class on Friday.

In addition I have had experience in training various other martial arts, so you will notice influences of, amongst others, Shaolin kung fu, karate, tai chi and kick-boxing.

I hope you have a great time at the training and for questions you can reach me at: merijn at ilyeo.nl

I enjoy Taekwondo as an ideal sport for fitness and health with a positive effect on endurance, flexibility, physical and mental strength, coordination, balance, as well as self-confidence, self-awareness and even your way of life...

I will take over the Monday training from 8:00 till 9:30 pm and I hope to be able to pass on my passion for Taekwondo also to you.
