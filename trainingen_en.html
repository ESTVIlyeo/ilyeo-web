Trainings

Join our training on:

Monday from 18:30 till 20:00 @ Hal 1
Thursday from 20:00 till 21:30 @ Hal 3
(in the TU/e sports centre)

On Friday from 20:30 till 22:00 there is an extra training for members who have a yellow belt or higher. This training usually very focused, for instance on training for exams or for a sparring competition.

To join the training you must have a sports card. Members who have a yellow belt or higher are expected to wear a dobok (traditional taekwondo clothes). Members with a white belt may wear long trousers such as sweatpants and a t-shirt. Please wear flipflops or some other kind of footwear when walking from the changing room to the training hall.

New member?

If you're interested in taekwondo you are welcome to join a training. The first few trainings you don't have to register as a member. Once you have decided that you want to join the association you have to register and pay the membership fee of €10 for a year.

Training program

All sorts of exercises are embedded in the trainings, taking place two times a week led by our first dan black belt trainer. A training is more or less constructed as follows:

  • Warming up: Running, jumping, power or balance-exercises combined with practising reflexes.
  • Flexibility; you will be amazed by your own flexibility as a result of the extensive stretching routines!
  • Kicking techniques: different kicking techniques are regarded consistently, rotated with the training of several muscle groups such as legs, arms/chest and stomach/back
  • The remaining content of every training differs: sparring, self-defence, combination kicks, techniques and endurance
  • Cooling down; often sparring, kicking or punching circuits are concluded with a cooling down. When finished we often hang around in the training hall for some time before meeting up again in sports cantina!
